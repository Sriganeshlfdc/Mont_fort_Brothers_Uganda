Where We Are

Explore our mission across Uganda through these vibrant communities.

Entebbe – Mpala

Located near Lake Victoria, our Mpala center focuses on early education and community outreach.

Jinja – Kyebando

In the heart of Jinja, Kyebando fosters spiritual growth and academic excellence for youth and families.

Fort Portal – Isunga

Surrounded by lush hills, Isunga is a beacon of faith-based learning and environmental stewardship.
